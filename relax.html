Relaxation Retreats in India

Experience Peace and Serenity

Goa Beaches

Location: Goa, India Duration: 3-4 days

Relax on the pristine beaches of Goa, with soft golden sands, clear blue waters, and vibrant nightlife.

Coorg – Coffee Plantations

Location: Coorg, Karnataka, India Duration: 2 days

Escape to the lush coffee plantations of Coorg, known for its scenic beauty and calm environment.

Alleppey – Kerala Backwaters

Location: Alleppey, Kerala, India Duration: 2-3 days

Experience a serene houseboat ride in Kerala's tranquil backwaters, surrounded by lush green landscapes.

Udaipur – Lakes and Palaces

Location: Udaipur, Rajasthan, India Duration: 2 days

Explore the calm lakes and majestic palaces of Udaipur, offering a peaceful and historic atmosphere.

Rishikesh – Yoga Retreats

Location: Rishikesh, Uttarakhand, India Duration: 3-5 days

Rishikesh is a hub for yoga and wellness, offering spiritual retreats and peaceful meditation spots.

Manali – Mountain Retreats

Location: Manali, Himachal Pradesh, India Duration: 3-4 days

Nestled in the mountains, Manali offers a peaceful retreat amidst snow-capped peaks, lush valleys, and serene landscapes.
